Welcome to whey to go!

Whey To Go is a small, family run cheesemongers based in the Heart of England.
We offer a range of cheeses that are sourced from around the world in our brilliantly located store.
Here on this website you can find a range of terribly cheesy recipes, learn more about the history of our cheeses
and discover cheese pairing suggestions for all occasions in our cheese index.



We also produce our own cheese! We run a partnership with local farmers who deliver us the freshest milk from down the road, which we then churn into cheeses. All products are crafted by hand, before they are stored in our historic cellar that is over 400 years old, located beneath our store. The cheeses mature in the cellar for up to 10 years before makong it upstairs and is ready to be sold to our loyal customers. Come and visit us instore to browse our fine selection of cheeses or book in and take a tour and learn about the history of our buisness.
